Courses:

BS-50

The BS50 course is designed as a comprehensive introduction to various disciplines within our academy, providing students with a broad foundation in key areas of study. Over the span of 12 weeks, participants will embark on an immersive journey where they will gain exposure to essential concepts across multiple domains. Multifaceted Learning Approach: The course adopts a multifaceted learning approach, offering insights into diverse subjects, ranging from programming languages to foundational concepts in computer science. Exploration of Programming Languages: Students will delve into programming languages such as C++, C#, and JavaScript, equipping them with versatile skills applicable across different domains of software development. Foundation in Programming Concepts: Through a series of engaging lectures and hands-on exercises, participants will build a solid foundation in programming concepts, including variables, control structures, functions, and data structures. Introduction to Web Development: The curriculum also includes an introduction to web development, where students will learn the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, enabling them to create dynamic and interactive web applications. Critical Thinking and Problem-Solving Skills: Throughout the course, emphasis is placed on fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills, empowering students to tackle real-world challenges with confidence and creativity. Preparation for Specialization: By the end of the BS50 course, students will have acquired a comprehensive understanding of various disciplines within the academy, laying the groundwork for them to make informed decisions about their future specialization. Practical Application and Project Work: Hands-on projects and practical exercises form an integral part of the curriculum, allowing students to apply their newfound knowledge in real-world scenarios and build a portfolio of impressive projects. Expert Guidance and Mentorship: Students will benefit from expert guidance and mentorship provided by experienced instructors, who are dedicated to supporting their learning journey and fostering a collaborative learning environment. Overall, the BS50 course serves as a springboard for students to explore the vast landscape of computer science and software development, providing them with the essential skills and knowledge needed to pursue further specialization and excel in their chosen field.

BS-60

BS60 is an introductory course into the realm of game development, designed to provide students with a broad overview of fundamental concepts and tools in this field. Over the course of 12 weeks, participants will be introduced to key aspects of game creation and programming basics. Key Features of the BS60 Course: Introduction to Game Development: The course begins with an overview of basic game development concepts, covering aspects such as game structure, gameplay mechanics, and the process of creating game content. Fundamentals of Programming: Students will receive introductory training in programming basics using languages like C++ and C#, as well as familiarization with object-oriented programming principles. Introduction to Game Engines: The course will provide a brief introduction to working with game engines such as Unity and Unreal Engine, enabling students to explore their functionality and capabilities. Hands-on Projects: A portion of the course will be dedicated to practical projects, allowing students to apply their newfound knowledge and create simple game prototypes. Understanding Career Opportunities: The course will also offer an overview of potential career paths in game development and provide advice on how to continue learning and advancing in this field. BS60 is intended to serve as the first step into the world of game development, providing students with foundational knowledge and skills necessary for further study and specialization in this exciting domain.

BS-61

BS61 is an advanced game development course tailored to those who have already completed the introductory BS60 course or have equivalent experience in game development. Over the duration of 48 weeks, students will delve deeper into the intricacies of game development, honing their skills and mastering advanced techniques to elevate their game projects to the next level. Key Features of the BS61 Course: Advanced Programming Concepts: Building upon the foundational programming knowledge acquired in BS60, students will explore more advanced programming concepts, including advanced data structures, algorithms, and optimization techniques tailored specifically for game development. Specialized Game Engine Training: BS61 provides in-depth training on specialized aspects of game engines such as Unity and Unreal Engine. Students will learn advanced features, optimization techniques, and best practices for leveraging the full potential of these powerful engines. Advanced Gameplay Mechanics: This course will focus on implementing complex gameplay mechanics, such as artificial intelligence, physics simulations, advanced animation systems, and interactive environments, to create immersive and engaging gaming experiences. Multiplayer Game Development: Students will learn the intricacies of developing multiplayer games, including networking protocols, synchronization techniques, and server-client architecture, to create dynamic and interactive multiplayer experiences. Project-Based Learning: BS61 emphasizes hands-on, project-based learning, where students will work on advanced game development projects under the guidance of experienced instructors. These projects will challenge students to apply their skills to solve real-world problems and develop polished game prototypes. Industry Insights and Best Practices: Throughout the course, students will gain valuable insights into industry trends, emerging technologies, and best practices in game development. Guest lectures, case studies, and industry projects will provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the current landscape of the game development industry. BS61 is designed to equip students with the advanced skills and knowledge required to succeed in the competitive field of game development. By the end of the course, students will have the expertise and portfolio necessary to pursue careers as professional game developers or to continue their education in specialized areas of game development.

BS-70

BS70 is an introductory course in web development aimed at providing students with a comprehensive understanding of essential web development concepts and tools. Over the span of 12 weeks, participants will embark on a journey to explore the dynamic world of web development, learning key languages, frameworks, and techniques to create interactive and engaging websites. Key Features of the BS70 Course: Foundations of Web Development: The course begins with an exploration of the foundational concepts of web development, covering topics such as the structure of the web, client-server architecture, and the role of web browsers. HTML, CSS, and JavaScript: Students will dive into the core technologies of web development, including HTML for structuring web pages, CSS for styling and layout, and JavaScript for adding interactivity and dynamic behavior to websites. Introduction to React Framework: BS70 introduces students to React, one of the most popular and powerful JavaScript frameworks for building user interfaces. Students will learn how to use React to create reusable components, manage state, and handle user interactions. Practical Application and Projects: Throughout the course, students will apply their newfound knowledge to practical projects, building real-world websites and web applications. These projects will enable students to showcase their skills and creativity while gaining hands-on experience in web development. Responsive Web Design: Students will learn the principles of responsive web design, including techniques for creating websites that adapt and display optimally across various devices and screen sizes, from desktop computers to mobile devices. BS70 is designed to equip students with the fundamental skills and knowledge needed to kickstart their careers in web development. Whether students aspire to become front-end developers, full-stack developers, or pursue further specialization in web development, this course provides a solid foundation for their journey in the dynamic and fast-paced world of web development.

BS-71

BS71 is an advanced web development course designed for students who have completed the introductory BS70 course or have equivalent experience in web development. Over the course of 25 weeks, students will delve into advanced technologies and tools in web development, expanding their skills and knowledge to create high-quality web applications. Key Features of the BS71 Course: Advanced Study of React: Students will continue their study of React, a JavaScript framework that enables the creation of powerful and interactive user interfaces. Within the course, they will deepen their knowledge by exploring advanced concepts and development methods using React. PHP Programming Language and Laravel Framework: Students will be introduced to the PHP programming language, widely used for creating dynamic web applications. They will also learn the Laravel framework, which provides a plethora of tools and features to expedite the web application development process. Containerization Technologies: Docker and Kubernetes: Throughout the course, students will master containerization technologies such as Docker and Kubernetes. They will learn how to create, manage, and scale containerized applications, ensuring their reliability and scalability. Advanced Projects and Practical Application: Students will apply their knowledge in practice by working on advanced web development projects. These projects will help them solidify their understanding and gain valuable experience in tackling real-world web development challenges. BS71 provides students with the opportunity for in-depth exploration of modern web development technologies and acquiring practical skills for a successful career in this field. Upon completion of the course, students will be ready to work on high-tech projects and apply advanced web development methodologies.

BS-80

The BS80 course is a comprehensive and in-depth overview of operating systems, covering both basic principles and advanced concepts. Over the course of 1.5 years, you will delve into the following topics: Fundamentals of Operating Systems: You will study the architecture of operating systems, processes and threads, memory management, and file systems. Network Operating Systems: Exploration of network interaction basics, protocols, and network operating system architectures. Operating System Security: Study of operating system security mechanisms, authentication, authorization, and data protection. Multitasking and Parallelism: Immersion into multitasking and parallelism concepts, including multithreaded programming and synchronization. Virtualization and Containerization: Overview of virtualization and containerization technologies such as VMware, Docker, and Kubernetes. Resource Management and Performance: Investigation of resource optimization methods and performance enhancement in operating systems. Distributed Systems: Study of distributed systems principles and distributed programming algorithms. Modern Trends in Operating Systems: Familiarization with the latest trends in operating systems development, such as cloud computing, IoT, and artificial intelligence. The BS80 course offers a deep dive into the world of operating systems, providing students with a thorough understanding and skills to work with modern systems.
